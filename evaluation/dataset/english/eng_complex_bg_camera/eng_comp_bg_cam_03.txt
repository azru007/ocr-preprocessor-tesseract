The Dream
Madhav had a dream that he had taken a loan of a hundred gold coins from his wealthy neighbour, Ramlal. He dreamt that he was spending the money on expensive clothes and tasty food. He bought himself a horse and was riding on it around town.
Even after waking up, he could not forget the dream.
He had neither enjoyed himself so much nor he had such a lovely dream ever in his life.
